About This Program

Pole Fitness Foundations

Pole Fitness – Foundations is a beginner-friendly class designed to introduce you to pole movement in a safe, structured environment. Through each curated, smooth, and authentic movement, you will build upper body strength, core stability, and coordination at a comfortable pace. No prior experience is required — this program is designed for absolute beginners who want to learn pole fitness from the ground up.

Beginner Pole Fitness Classes on the Gold Coast — Safe, Empowering Training at UNDRGRND Movement Southport

Pole fitness is one of the most effective and empowering full-body workouts available to adults on the Gold Coast. Far removed from its entertainment origins, modern pole fitness is recognised internationally as a competitive sport and athletic discipline that develops extraordinary levels of strength, flexibility, coordination, and body confidence. At UNDRGRND Movement in Southport, our Pole Fitness Foundations class is designed to introduce Gold Coast adults to this remarkable discipline in a safe, structured, and completely beginner-friendly environment.

What Is Pole Fitness?

Pole fitness is an athletic discipline performed on a vertical pole, combining elements of gymnastics, dance, and strength training. Students learn to grip, walk, spin, and eventually hold and invert on the pole using full-body strength and technique. It is practised competitively at national and international levels, and is increasingly recognised as one of the most comprehensive strength and conditioning workouts available.

On the Gold Coast, pole fitness has grown significantly in popularity as adults seek fitness experiences that are challenging, creative, and community-oriented. UNDRGRND Movement's Southport studio offers a structured, sport-focused approach to pole fitness that is accessible to complete beginners and progressively challenging for those who continue to develop their skills.

Benefits of Pole Fitness Training

The physical benefits of regular pole fitness practice are comprehensive and well-documented. Full-body strength is the most immediate result — particularly in the upper body, core, and grip. The demands of supporting your own body weight on the pole develop functional strength that transfers directly to everyday movement and other athletic pursuits. Gold Coast students who train consistently report significant improvements in pull-up strength, core stability, and overall body composition.

Core stability is a defining feature of pole fitness training. Every movement on the pole requires active engagement of the deep core muscles — the muscles that support the spine, stabilise the pelvis, and protect the lower back. Regular pole fitness training at our Southport studio develops core strength that has wide-ranging benefits for posture, injury prevention, and athletic performance.

Flexibility improves naturally through consistent pole fitness practice. The range of motion required for pole movements progressively stretches the hips, hamstrings, shoulders, and spine. Unlike static stretching, the flexibility gains from pole fitness are dynamic and functional, meaning they support active movement rather than passive range of motion alone.

Pole Fitness vs. Pole Dancing — Understanding the Difference

One of the most common misconceptions about pole fitness is that it is the same as pole dancing. It is not. Pole fitness is an athletic, strength-based discipline that is taught and practised as a sport. At UNDRGRND Movement's Southport studio, we teach pole as a fitness and movement discipline with a focus on technique, control, and progressive physical development. The emphasis is entirely on what your body can do — not on performance or entertainment.

This distinction matters for Gold Coast adults who are curious about pole fitness but have been put off by misconceptions. Our Pole Fitness Foundations class is welcoming to all genders, all body types, and all fitness backgrounds. The only requirement is a willingness to learn and a commitment to showing up. Many of our Gold Coast students describe their first pole fitness class as one of the most surprisingly enjoyable and empowering experiences they have had in a fitness setting.

Safe Progression for Absolute Beginners

The UNDRGRND Movement approach to teaching pole fitness is built on the principle of progressive, safety-first skill development. Every student in the Foundations class starts from the absolute beginning — grip technique, body positioning, and controlled walking around the pole. No movement is introduced until the preceding skill has been properly established. This approach ensures that students develop genuine competence and confidence at each stage before progressing to the next.

Our Southport instructors are experienced in teaching beginners and are skilled at adapting their instruction to individual students' needs, fitness levels, and learning styles. Gold Coast students of all ages and backgrounds have successfully completed our Pole Fitness Foundations program, and many have gone on to progress through our intermediate and advanced pole programs.

Why Pole Fitness Is an Incredible Workout

Pole fitness is one of the few fitness disciplines that develops strength, flexibility, coordination, and body awareness simultaneously. A single 60-minute class at our Southport studio will challenge your grip strength, upper body endurance, core stability, and spatial awareness in ways that conventional gym workouts simply cannot replicate. The progressive nature of the skill development also means that there is always something new to work towards — keeping motivation high and progress visible.

Gold Coast students who train regularly in pole fitness report improvements not just in their physical fitness, but in their overall relationship with their body. The process of learning to do something physically challenging — something that initially seemed impossible — builds a kind of confidence and self-belief that extends far beyond the studio.
