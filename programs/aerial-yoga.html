What to Expect in Class

A typical Aerial Yoga class at UNDRGRND Movement Southport

Hammock Introduction (10 min)

Learn how to use the silk hammock safely — how to sit, stand, and support your weight. First-timers get extra guidance.

Grounded Warm-Up (10 min)

A gentle warm-up on the mat to prepare the body before transitioning into the hammock.

Aerial Yoga Practice (30 min)

Guided yoga sequences using the hammock for support, deepening, and inversion. All levels catered for.

Restorative Cool-Down (10 min)

Deeply restorative hammock positions — including a supported savasana — to close the session.

Aerial Yoga Classes on the Gold Coast — Silk Hammock Yoga at UNDRGRND Movement Southport

UNDRGRND Movement offers Aerial Yoga classes at our Southport studio, 163 Ferry Rd, Gold Coast. This beginner-friendly program uses silk hammocks to support the body in yoga postures, making inversions accessible, deepening stretches, and providing the unique benefit of spinal decompression.

What Is Aerial Yoga?

Aerial Yoga at UNDRGRND Movement is a yoga practice performed using a silk hammock suspended from the ceiling. The hammock acts as a supportive tool — allowing Gold Coast students to explore yoga postures with greater depth, access inversions safely, and experience the deeply restorative effects of supported suspension.

At UNDRGRND Movement Southport, Aerial Yoga classes are designed for beginners. No prior aerial or yoga experience is required. Our instructor guides each class carefully, ensuring every Gold Coast student learns to use the hammock safely and confidently from their very first session.

The Benefits of Aerial Yoga on the Gold Coast

Aerial Yoga at UNDRGRND Movement Southport offers benefits that mat yoga alone cannot provide. Spinal decompression in suspended positions relieves tension accumulated from sitting, standing, and daily movement. Inversions — positions where the head is below the heart — are made safe and accessible by the hammock's support. Deep stretches are achieved more quickly and safely than on the mat. And the unique challenge of maintaining balance in the hammock builds deep core strength in a way that is both effective and enjoyable.
