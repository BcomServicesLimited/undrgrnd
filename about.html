About UNDRGRND Movement

UNDRGRND Movement stands for foundational movement for body and mind. Not competition. Not performance pressure. Personal transformation only.

Our Philosophy

Movement for Body and Mind

At UNDRGRND Movement, we believe that movement is personal, powerful, and transformative. Whether you're discovering dance for the first time, building strength through pole fitness, finding peace in yoga, or defying gravity with aerial arts, your journey is uniquely yours.

Not Competition

We don't compare. We don't compete. Your only measure is yourself — yesterday vs. today. Every body moves differently, and that's exactly how it should be. At our Gold Coast studio, you'll never feel judged for where you are in your journey.

Personal Transformation

Movement changes you. Physically, yes — but also mentally and emotionally. We create space for you to discover strength you didn't know you had and reconnect with your body on your terms. Southport and the Gold Coast deserve a studio built around real transformation.

Every Body Welcome

Beginners, recovering athletes, those returning after injury, people just looking to try something new — you all belong here. No judgment. No pressure. Just support. Our Gold Coast community is built on inclusion, and we mean it.

Our Approach

Movement Beyond Four Walls

Unlike traditional studios, we take movement to where it makes sense — not just indoors, but outdoors, by the ocean, in nature. The Gold Coast is one of the most naturally beautiful places in Australia, and we believe movement should connect with that environment, not ignore it.

Our Southport Home Base

UNDRGRND Movement — fully equipped with poles, aerial rigging, yoga space, and everything needed for safe, professional training. Our Gold Coast studio is purpose-built for the full range of movement disciplines we offer.

Ocean-Side Sessions

Selected classes held at Gold Coast beaches. Movement with sand beneath your feet, salt air in your lungs, and the ocean as your soundtrack. There is no better setting for grounding movement practice than the Gold Coast coastline.

Outdoor Movement

Park sessions in natural settings across the Gold Coast. Grounding, energising, and connecting with your body in open space. Outdoor movement practice has been shown to enhance both physical and mental wellbeing beyond what indoor training alone can achieve.

Research-backed approach: Supported by World Health Organization and International Association for Dance Medicine & Science (IADMS) recommendations for varied training environments and holistic movement practice.

UNDRGRND Movement: Gold Coast's Inclusive Movement Studio

UNDRGRND Movement is a Gold Coast movement studio based in Southport, offering dance, pole fitness, yoga, aerial arts, and recovery movement classes for adults and children. Founded on a philosophy of personal transformation over performance, UNDRGRND Movement has created a space on the Gold Coast where every body is genuinely welcome, regardless of age, fitness level, or prior experience.

A Different Kind of Gold Coast Studio

Most fitness studios on the Gold Coast are built around performance metrics, competition, and visible results. UNDRGRND Movement takes a fundamentally different approach. UNDRGRND Movement, the studio was designed from the ground up to serve people who have never exercised before, people returning from injury, and people who simply want to move their body without judgment or pressure.

This approach is not just a marketing position — it is embedded in every class structure, every instructor qualification, and every policy at the studio. Trauma-informed movement principles guide the teaching methodology, ensuring that the Gold Coast community has access to movement education that genuinely respects the individual.

Serving Southport and the Gold Coast

Situated in Southport, UNDRGRND Movement is conveniently located for residents across the Gold Coast, including Surfers Paradise, Broadbeach, Main Beach, Labrador, Bundall, Arundel, Parkwood, and Molendinar. The studio's location on Ferry Rd provides easy access from the Southport CBD and surrounding Gold Coast suburbs, with street parking available.

Programs for Every Stage of Life

UNDRGRND Movement offers 17 foundation programs for both adults and children on the Gold Coast. Adult programs include Booty Burn - Foundations, Movement Flow - Foundations, Choreography Fusion - Foundations, Fusion Yoga - Foundations, Aerial Silks - Foundations, Stretch & Mobility - Foundations, Aerial Yoga - Foundations (coming soon), Pole Fitness - Foundations (coming soon), Pole Flow - Foundations (coming soon), and Recovery Movement Flow - Foundations (coming soon). Kids programs span ages 4 to 16 and include dance, yoga, pole, and aerial disciplines tailored to each age group.

For adults, programs are grouped by intensity — Dynamic (16–25 intensity), Balanced (26–45 intensity), and Gentle (45+ intensity) — to help students find classes that match their energy and goals on the Gold Coast. For kids, flexible age bands (6–9 combined, 10–15 combined) ensure appropriate grouping at the Southport studio. Every program carries the “Foundations” designation, meaning it is designed for complete beginners with no prior experience required.

All programs are available on a drop-in basis, as class packs, or as unlimited monthly memberships — giving Gold Coast students the flexibility to engage with movement in a way that suits their lifestyle and budget. No lock-in contracts. No pressure to commit before you are ready.

Movement Beyond the Studio

One of the most distinctive features of UNDRGRND Movement is its commitment to taking movement beyond the studio walls. Selected classes are held at Gold Coast beaches and in outdoor parks, connecting movement practice with the natural environment that makes the Gold Coast one of the most desirable places to live in Australia. This multi-environment approach is supported by research from the World Health Organization and the International Association for Dance Medicine and Science, both of which recommend varied training environments for optimal physical and mental wellbeing.

Safety, Qualifications, and Professional Standards

UNDRGRND Movement takes safety seriously across all disciplines. All instructors hold relevant certifications, including yoga registration, pole and aerial rigging safety qualifications, First Aid certification, and Working with Children Checks. The studio is fully insured for all class types and environments, and all teaching methodology is grounded in internationally recognised movement and somatic therapy standards.
