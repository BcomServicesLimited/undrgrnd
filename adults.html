Adult Movement Classes on the Gold Coast — Dance, Pole, Yoga & Aerial at UNDRGRND Movement Southport

UNDRGRND Movement in Southport offers beginner-friendly adult classes across dance, pole fitness, yoga, and aerial disciplines on the Gold Coast. Our foundation programs are designed for complete beginners, with intensity levels to match your energy and goals. Whether you are 16 or 65, returning after years away or stepping into a studio for the first time, our Gold Coast classes meet you exactly where you are.

Dynamic / High Energy Classes (16-25 Young Adults)

For adults seeking fast-paced, energetic movement on the Gold Coast, our Dynamic classes deliver. Booty Burn - Foundations targets lower body strength through groove-based movement — a fun, music-driven class that works glutes, hips, and core while developing coordination and body confidence. No prior dance experience is required. This class at our Southport studio is open to adults of all fitness levels across the Gold Coast.

Balanced / Flow Classes (26-45 General Adults)

Our Balanced intensity classes suit adults seeking moderate-paced movement with emphasis on technique and flow. Movement Flow - Foundations teaches natural movement principles for body confidence and expressive movement. Pole Fitness - Foundations and Pole Flow - Foundations (both coming soon to our Southport studio) introduce pole movement safely, building grip technique, body positioning, and flowing transitions. Adults from across the Gold Coast, including Surfers Paradise, Broadbeach, and Bundall, regularly attend our pole fitness classes.

Our yoga and movement offerings each bring different approaches to building strength, flexibility, and body awareness on the Gold Coast. Fusion Yoga - Foundations blends traditional yoga with contemporary movement for a dynamic, accessible practice. Aerial Yoga - Foundations (coming soon) uses suspended silk hammocks for a unique, decompressive practice. Aerial Silks - Foundations builds upper body strength, core stability, and spatial awareness through suspended fabric work. Choreography Fusion - Foundations builds coordination and confidence through accessible, structured dance sequences. Stretch & Mobility - Foundations is a gentle, beginner-friendly class focused on improving flexibility, reducing stiffness, and restoring ease of movement at UNDRGRND Movement Southport.

Gentle / Slow Classes (45+ Recovery)

Recovery Movement Flow - Foundations (coming soon) supports safe return to movement after injury or extended breaks. This gentle class in Southport requires medical clearance and focuses on slow, controlled sequences that rebuild trust in your body. Perfect for adults on the Gold Coast recovering from injury, managing physical limitations, or simply seeking a more supportive and mindful approach to movement. UNDRGRND Movement is one of the only Gold Coast studios to offer a dedicated recovery movement program — treating movement as both a physical and emotional practice.

Why Choose UNDRGRND Movement on the Gold Coast

Every adult program at our Southport studio starts with foundations — no experience required. We keep classes small (maximum 15 students) to ensure every adult on the Gold Coast receives individual attention and instruction. Our qualified instructors build structured, progressive programs that develop real movement skills at a pace that works for each person. We do not run competitive classes. We do not push students beyond their limits. Instead, we create a welcoming, non-judgmental environment where adults of all ages and abilities can discover what their bodies are capable of.

Book your first class at UNDRGRND Movement Southport and discover movement that feels good. From dance to pole to yoga to aerial to stretching, we will help you build strength, confidence, and a sustainable practice on the Gold Coast.

Serving Adults Across the Gold Coast

UNDRGRND Movement's Southport studio is accessible to adults from across the Gold Coast, including Surfers Paradise, Broadbeach, Bundall, Main Beach, Nerang, Robina, and surrounding suburbs. Our timetable is designed to suit working adults, with morning, afternoon, and evening sessions available throughout the week.
