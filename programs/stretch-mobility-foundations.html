      About Stretch & Mobility - Foundations

      Stretch & Mobility – Foundations is a beginner-friendly class focused on improving flexibility, reducing stiffness, and restoring ease of movement. This class combines guided stretching with simple mobility work to help open up tight areas such as hips, hamstrings, back, and shoulders.

      Movements are slow, controlled, and accessible, allowing you to gradually increase range without forcing or pushing. Each session is designed to help you feel more relaxed, move more freely, and support recovery from training or daily stress. Ideal for adults who want to improve flexibility, reduce tightness, and feel better in their body.

      What to Expect in Class

      A typical Stretch & Mobility – Foundations class at UNDRGRND Movement Southport

      Gentle Warm-Up (10 min)

      A slow, guided warm-up to bring awareness into the body and prepare your joints and muscles for deeper stretching.

      Targeted Mobility Work (20 min)

      Focused mobility exercises for key areas — hips, hamstrings, thoracic spine, and shoulders — using controlled, supported movements.

      Guided Stretching Sequences (20 min)

      Slow, held stretches that allow the body to release tension gradually. Each stretch is explained clearly with modifications available.

      Cool-Down and Rest (10 min)

      A calm, restorative close to the session. You will leave feeling relaxed, open, and more at ease in your body.

      Frequently Asked Questions

      No experience is needed. Stretch & Mobility – Foundations at UNDRGRND Movement Southport is designed for complete beginners. Every movement is explained clearly and modifications are always available. If you can breathe, you can do this class.

      This class is gentle and low-impact, making it suitable for many people managing tightness or recovering from minor injuries. Please inform your instructor of any injuries or conditions before class so they can offer appropriate modifications. If you have a serious injury or medical condition, consult your healthcare provider first.

      While yoga incorporates stretching, Stretch & Mobility – Foundations is purely focused on improving range of motion, releasing tension, and restoring ease of movement. There is no spiritual component, no complex poses, and no requirement to hold challenging positions. It is straightforward, accessible, and results-focused.

      Wear comfortable clothing you can move freely in. A yoga mat is recommended — we have mats available at the studio if needed. Bring water and arrive a few minutes early for your first session so your instructor can welcome you.

      Most participants notice a difference in how they feel after just a few sessions. For lasting improvements in flexibility and mobility, attending once or twice per week consistently is recommended. Simply book each class as you go — no packs or memberships required.
