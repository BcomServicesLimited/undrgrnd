Medical Clearance Requirements

Medical clearance from your healthcare provider is required before participating in Recovery Movement classes. By booking this class, you confirm that you have obtained appropriate medical clearance and that your healthcare provider has approved your participation in physical movement activities.

The following requirements apply to all participants in Recovery Movement classes:

  • Written clearance required: Medical clearance must be provided in writing from a registered General Practitioner (GP) or specialist.
  • Currency of clearance: The clearance document must be dated within the three months prior to your first Recovery Movement class.
  • Documentation at first class: You must bring your written clearance documentation to your first Recovery Movement class. Digital copies are accepted.
  • Right to refuse entry: UNDGRND Movement instructors reserve the right to refuse entry to any Recovery Movement class where appropriate medical clearance has not been provided or cannot be verified.

If you are unsure whether you require medical clearance for any class, please contact us before booking. We are happy to discuss your circumstances and help you find the most appropriate class for your current health status.
