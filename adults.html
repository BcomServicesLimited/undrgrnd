Adult Dance Classes on the Gold Coast

Our adult dance programs are among the most popular offerings at our Southport studio. Afro Groove Foundations is a beginner-friendly dance fitness class inspired by Afrobeat and groove-based movement styles. It builds rhythm, coordination, lower body strength, and core stability through simple, repeatable patterns. No prior dance experience is required. Movement Dance takes a more expressive approach, helping students build body flow and confidence through creative movement exploration. Both classes are open to adults of all fitness levels across the Gold Coast.

For adults interested in choreography, our Choreography Fusion class blends movement and confidence-building through structured dance sequences. This class is particularly popular among women on the Gold Coast who want to explore movement as a form of self-expression in a supportive, non-competitive environment.

Pole Fitness Classes for Adults in Southport

Pole fitness is one of the most effective full-body workouts available to adults on the Gold Coast. Our Pole Fitness Foundations class is designed for complete beginners and teaches grip techniques, basic spins, body positioning, and safety protocols in a structured, supportive environment. As students progress, they can move into Pole Flow — developing smooth transitions and flowing movement — and Pole Strength and Movement, which focuses on conditioning, holds, and controlled technique.

All pole fitness classes at our Southport studio are taught by qualified instructors with a strong emphasis on safety and individual progression. Adults from across the Gold Coast, including Surfers Paradise and Broadbeach, regularly attend our pole fitness classes. No prior experience is needed to begin, and our instructors ensure every student feels comfortable and supported from their very first class.

Recovery Movement — Specialised Adult Classes in Southport

UNDRGRND Movement is one of the only Gold Coast studios to offer a dedicated Recovery Movement Dance program for adults. This specialised class supports individuals returning to movement after injury, surgery, or extended periods of physical or emotional stress. Using slow, mindful movement with controlled range of motion, the program provides a safe, supported pathway back to physical activity. Medical clearance from a healthcare provider is required before participating.

Our recovery movement approach is trauma-informed and developed in collaboration with registered counsellors. This makes UNDRGRND Movement a genuinely unique offering on the Gold Coast — a studio that treats movement as both a physical and emotional practice, with the professional support structures to match.
